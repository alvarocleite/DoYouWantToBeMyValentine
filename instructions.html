How to Personalize

You can personalize the message and the theme of the page directly in the URL!

1. Add a Name

Add ?name= followed by the name at the end of the URL.

?name=Sarah

2. Choose a Theme/Type

Add &type=marry to change the question to "Will You Marry Me?" and use golden colors.

&type=marry

Example:

A personalized proposal for Sarah:

https://alvarocleite.github.io/DoYouWantToBeMyValentine/?name=Sarah&type=marry

Try it out and send it to someone special!
